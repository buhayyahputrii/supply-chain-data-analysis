Supply Chain Data Analysis using Microsoft Excel

This project demonstrates how Microsoft Excel can be used to analyze supply chain data and generate actionable business insights for inventory management and operational decision-making.

The analysis focuses on identifying inventory risks, evaluating product demand, and assessing supplier performance using Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts.

---

Project Objectives

- Identify products with low inventory levels.
- Analyze product demand patterns.
- Compare supplier lead time performance.
- Support inventory planning through data-driven insights.

---

Tools

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Data Cleaning
- Data Analysis
- Inventory Analysis

---

Analysis Performed

Low Stock Risk

Identified products with relatively low inventory levels that may require replenishment to reduce stock-out risk.

 Demand Analysis

Analyzed sales volume to identify high-demand products and support inventory planning.

Supplier Lead Time

Compared supplier lead times to evaluate delivery performance and operational efficiency.

---

Business Insights

- Products with low inventory can be prioritized for replenishment.
- High-demand products require better inventory planning.
- Supplier lead time analysis helps evaluate procurement performance.
- Excel dashboards simplify operational reporting and decision-making.
